🚀 Getting Started

Mindflow is a new approach to project management that puts you first. Instead of managing tasks, you focus on outcomes - what success looks like. Your AI Buddy helps you think, not just do.

Quick Setup

# Clone and install
cd mindflow-dev
bun install

# Set your API key for AI chat
export OPENAI_API_KEY="your-key-here"

# Start the server
bun run dev

# In another terminal, serve the web UI
bunx serve web -p 3080

# Open in browser
open http://localhost:3080
Tip: Without an API key, the chat will still work but won't have full AI responses. All other features (outcomes, graph, settings) work without an API key.

💬 Chat with Buddy

Your AI Buddy is your thinking partner, not just an assistant. Talk naturally about what you're working on, and Buddy will help you clarify your thoughts.

Quick Actions

  • Quick thought - Capture a fleeting idea before you forget it
  • New outcome - Start defining what success looks like
  • I'm stuck - Get help when you hit a blocker

What You Can Say

  • "I want to launch my product by next month"
  • "I'm feeling overwhelmed with all these tasks"
  • "Help me break down this big project"
  • "What should I focus on today?"
  • "I'm stuck on the authentication flow"
Tip: Your Buddy adapts to your communication style. Set it in Settings to get responses that match how you think.

🎯 Outcomes

Outcomes are the heart of Mindflow. Instead of listing tasks, you describe what success looks like. This shift in thinking helps you stay focused on what matters.

Creating an Outcome

  1. Click New Outcome or press Cmd+N
  2. Describe what success looks like
  3. Explain why it matters (motivation)
  4. Choose a horizon (Now, Soon, Later, Someday)
  5. Optionally add success criteria

Horizons Explained

  • Now - This week. Your current focus.
  • Soon - This month. Coming up next.
  • Later - This quarter. On the roadmap.
  • Someday - No deadline. Dreams and ideas.

Outcome Statuses

  • Exploring - Still figuring out what this means
  • Committed - Ready to work on this
  • In Progress - Actively working on it
  • Blocked - Stuck and need help
  • Review - Ready for verification
  • Achieved - Success! You did it!

🕸 Mind Graph

The Mind Graph shows how all your thoughts and outcomes connect. It's a visual representation of your thinking.

Node Types

  • Outcomes - What you're trying to achieve
  • Thoughts - Ideas and observations
  • Actions - Things to do
  • Blockers - What's stopping you
  • Decisions - Choices you've made
  • People - Team members and collaborators

Using the Graph

  • Click on any node to see its details
  • Use the filter dropdown to show only specific types
  • The graph automatically arranges based on connections
  • Larger nodes are more important (outcomes, decisions)

⚙ Settings

Your Buddy

  • Buddy name - What to call your AI companion
  • Your name - So Buddy can address you personally

Communication Style

  • Direct - Skip the fluff, get to the point
  • Detailed - Provide context and explanations
  • Visual - Use lists, bullets, and structure

Work Style

  • Deep focus - Long uninterrupted work blocks
  • Context switching - Flexible between tasks
  • Collaborative - Work better with others

Neurodivergent Support

  • ADHD mode - Breaks tasks into smaller chunks, celebrates wins, helps maintain focus
  • Anxiety aware - Gentle pacing, avoids urgency language, validates feelings

Work Patterns

  • Max commitments - How many active outcomes to allow (prevents overload)

💡 Philosophy

"Humans think, Buddies coordinate"

Mindflow is built on four core principles:

1. Outcomes Over Tasks

Traditional project management focuses on tasks - what to do. Mindflow focuses on outcomes - what success looks like. This shift helps you stay focused on what actually matters instead of getting lost in busywork.

2. Horizons Over Deadlines

Deadlines create anxiety. Horizons create clarity. Instead of "due Friday at 5pm", you think "this is a Now priority" or "this is for Later". It's more human, less stressful.

3. Buddies Over Bosses

Your AI Buddy isn't here to manage you or check up on your work. It's a thinking partner that helps you clarify your thoughts and stay on track. No surveillance, just support.

4. Graphs Over Lists

Real work isn't linear. Ideas connect, outcomes depend on each other, decisions cascade. The Mind Graph shows these relationships instead of hiding them in flat lists.

Remember: Mindflow is a tool to help you think, not to think for you. The Oracle keeps the human human.
